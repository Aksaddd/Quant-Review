## Role Overview

Subject matter experts responsible for writing, reviewing, and tagging all quant interview problems, flashcard concepts, and formula definitions. Works from the **Technique Atlas taxonomy** defined by the Curriculum Lead.

> Must have a **quantitative finance background** — content covering probability, stochastic processes, brain teasers, and financial derivatives must be technically accurate at a hedge-fund interview standard.

## Core Responsibilities

- Author new problems across all chapters (Probability, Statistics, Brain Teasers, Stochastic Processes, Finance, Coding)
- Tag every problem with technique type, prerequisite concepts, and difficulty rating per the **Technique Atlas schema**
- Write **progressive hint sequences** (3-level hint ladders) for every new problem
- Author **formula flashcards** with memory palace encoding suggestions (vivid concrete images per Foer's Baker/baker principle)
- Write **concept flashcards** with elaborative encoding anchors — associate abstract terms to concrete, memorable images
- Review and QA all AI-generated problem variants for mathematical accuracy
- Write the **"Fun Failure" explanations** for each problem: what the wrong approach was, what the correct technique is
- Produce **"Express Mode" solution write-ups** in clear, student-facing language (no unexplained LaTeX walls)
