## Role Overview

Owns **distribution, community, and the social connectivity layer** that McGonigal identifies as one of the four core intrinsic rewards. Responsible for getting the platform in front of quant candidates, building the Discord/community flywheel, managing social channels, and running user research interviews that feed back to the product team.

> The platform's **epic meaning layer** — percentile rankings, collective milestones — depends on this person driving community density.

---

## Core Responsibilities

- Own all distribution channels: **LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Reddit** (r/quant, r/cscareerquestions, r/math), **Discord**
- Build and moderate the platform's **Discord community** — weekly study groups, problem-of-the-week threads
- Run **bi-weekly user interviews**: recruit from active user base, synthesize insights for Curriculum Lead and CTO
- Manage **referral and word-of-mouth programs** — design incentive structure for student referrers
- Produce **weekly content**: problem walkthroughs, quant interview tips, memory technique tutorials (LinkedIn/Twitter)
- Track acquisition metrics: **CAC, channel attribution, activation rate, referral conversion**
- Own the **Epic Meaning copy**: leaderboard descriptions, collective milestone announcements, percentile rank messaging
- Partner with quant finance clubs at target universities: **Columbia, NYU, MIT, UChicago, CMU**
