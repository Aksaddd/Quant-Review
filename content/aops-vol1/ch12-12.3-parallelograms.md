If both pairs of opposite sides of ABCD are parallel, then ABCD is a parallelogram. By drawing the diagonals of a parallelogram, we learn a lot more. Because the opposite sides of ABCD are parallel, we have /CAB = /ACD and IBCA = IDAC. By ASA, we have A ABC = A CDA, so BC = DA and AB = CD. Also we find /ABC = iCDA. Similarly we can show that / BCD = /.DAB. Thus, we have shown that the opposite angles of parallelograms are equal, as are the opposite sides.
