Regular Polygons

Most polygons you will encounter in geometry problems which have more than four sides will be regular polygons. As we stated before, the angles of a regular polygon are all equal, so knowing the sum of the interior angles and the sum of the exterior angles, we can determine the measure of each angle in a regular polygon.
