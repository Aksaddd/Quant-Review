Rhombuses (Rhombi?)

If all four sides of a quadrilateral are equal then the quadrilateral is a rhombus. By drawing the diagonals of a rhombus as shown, we can quickly show that any rhombus is also a parallelogram. By SSS congruency, we have AABD = ACDB, so lABD = iCDB and lADB = iCBD. Thus AB ||CD and BC ||DA, so ABCD is a parallelogram.
